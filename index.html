About chaniru.arw

I shoot for the moments you'll want to feel again, not just see again.

I'm Chaniru - a Sri Lanka based photographer working under the name chaniru.arw. My frames lean toward natural light, real reactions and the small unscripted seconds that usually get missed: a shared glance, a nervous laugh, a hand being held a little tighter.

Whether it's a full wedding weekend, an engagement shoot at golden hour, or a quiet portrait session, my approach stays the same - stay close, stay out of the way, and let the day tell its own story.
